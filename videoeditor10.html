Video Editor 10

About Project

Video Editor 10 is a powerful video editor for Windows with a simple and easy-to-use user interface. It gives you lots of advanced features that you can work into your videos, including moving graphics, text overlays, and gorgeous filters.

Video Editor 10 has all the advanced features that professional video editors expect but also makes it easy for beginners to get started.
